About Me

I bring over 18 years of experience in the pharmaceutical industry, where I’ve led large-scale clinical trials, managed global regulatory start-ups, and helped drive new therapies to market across multiple therapeutic areas. My core strengths lie in cross-functional leadership, regulatory navigation, and clinical operations execution — with a deep understanding of drug development from early phase through to post-marketing studies.

Beyond my core profession, I have a strong personal interest in artificial intelligence and data science, particularly in how they can enhance clinical trial design and real-world evidence analytics. In my own time, I explore projects involving machine learning, biomarker data interpretation, and dashboard development. These technical pursuits reflect my curiosity and passion for innovation, complementing my domain expertise in clinical research.
